Load and follow the `no-slop` skill, then sweep your own writing this session.

1. List the prose files you created or edited this session (`.md`, `.mdx`, `.txt`, `.rst`).
2. For each, scan for AI tells: throat-clearing ("it's worth noting that", "needless to say", "first and foremost", "when it comes to"), stock openers ("in today's fast-paced world", "in the realm of", "the world of"), decorative verbs ("delve into", "dive deep into", "navigate the complexities", "embark on a journey"), marketing slop ("unlock the full potential", "seamlessly integrate", "a game-changer", "elevate your", "robust and scalable", "ever-evolving"), faux-literary phrasing ("rich tapestry", "a testament to", "underscores the importance of"), "furthermore" + "moreover" stacked together, and pile-ups of em-dashes in a short paragraph.
3. For each hit, **rewrite it plainly**: cut the decoration and keep the plain claim underneath. The result should be shorter. One idea per sentence, no throat-clearing, no transitions for their own sake. If a sentence carries no idea once stripped, delete it.
4. If a tell is there on purpose (quoting it as an example, reproducing source copy verbatim), keep it and record one line: `NO-SLOP: INTENTIONAL — <reason>`.
5. Report what you rewrote and anything you deliberately kept as intentional.

Don't say "done" while AI slop is still standing in for plain writing.
